The Problem with Traditional Sports Entertainment

Limited Engagement

Fans are mostly spectators. Their involvement is limited to cheering, watching, and buying merchandise.

No Ownership

 Fans have no tangible way of sharing in their team's success. They can't directly benefit from a team’s victories or overall performance.

Betting Pitfalls

Sports betting is a high-risk activity with addictive tendencies, offering little control or sense of investment beyond the immediate outcome of a game.
